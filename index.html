Abilitati

IMG Name Description
- Fissure Slams the ground with a mighty totem, fissuring the earth while stunning and damaging enemy units in a line. Creates an impassable ridge of stone.
- Enchant Totem Empowers Earthshaker's totem, causing it to deal extra damage on the next attack.
- Aftershock Causes the earth to shake underfoot, adding additional damage and stuns to nearby enemy units when Earthshaker casts his abilities.
- Echo Slam Shockwaves travel through the ground, damaging enemy units. Each enemy hit causes an echo to damage nearby units. Real heroes cause two echoes.
